# The Reach

The founding idea of this project. Origin: journaling sessions gathering
data on smoking, drinking, diet, and sleep.

## What it is

An afternoon pull. It shapeshifts:

- masks as hunger
- masks as wanting alcohol
- masks as coping with loneliness

## How it moves

Fast escalation, one small yes at a time:

> Taco Bell Chalupa → Burger → One Beer at the Bar

Strongest after a morning of a ton of coffee and cigarettes — the morning
writes the afternoon's script.

## The chronoVoyant answer

If future time could be *felt* now — how tomorrow feels after the workout
vs. after the Chalupa — the decision in the moment of The Reach would make
itself.

## Song directions (raw)

- The Reach as a character / a voice that knows your weak hours
- A song that takes place entirely between 2pm and 6pm
- The escalation itself as song structure: each verse one step further
  (chalupa verse, burger verse, bar verse)
- A duet between present self and future self
- The morning-after as a letter mailed backward in time
