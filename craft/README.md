# craft/ — Craft Mechanics

> "These are complex but objective — the trading-indicator layer of
> songwriting." — The Bible

The measurable mechanics of a lyric and an arrangement: prosody, rhyme,
syllable counts, structure proportions, harmony. None of this makes a
song good. All of it can make a good song fail, silently, if it's off.

Craft checks are diagnostics, not rules. A finding here is information —
"this stressed syllable fights the beat" — and what you do with it is a
Feel decision. (If you keep it, log it in `specs/deviation-log.md` only
when it breaks a spec non-negotiable; craft findings alone need no
permission slip.)
