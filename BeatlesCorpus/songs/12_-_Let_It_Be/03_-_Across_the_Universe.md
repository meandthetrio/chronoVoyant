## Why this melody works (axiom cross-reference)

- **Verse**: front-heavy perpetual eighths sitting on 7-over-I
  (melancholy against stability), sighing 2-note slurs, cadence
  suspended on 2 over V7 — instability saved for the *ends* of
  phrases: motion that never arrives.
- **Mantra**: back-heavy, slurred, rising to 5 — pure, open, floating
  (the 5-axis) — the released breath after the verse's stream.
- **Refrain**: five repeated 2s and a 3→1 fall — the most stable,
  narrow, front-heavy phrase in the song, carrying the claim of
  stasis; resolution (1) withheld over V, granted over IV→I.
- Total vocal range ≈ one octave (1 up to 7); no melisma except the
  mantra; almost entirely stepwise — the "endless stream" texture is a
  melodic decision, not only a lyrical image.
